How it works

Simple. Trackable. Effective.

Three components. One closed loop. Every tap is logged, attributed, and reported back to you.

1
📍
We place NFC cards everywhere tourists are
Elegant NFC cards go in taxis (headrests), hotel rooms, and Airbnb apartments across the city. Cards are live within 48 hours of signup.
2
📲
Tourist taps — and lands on your business
One tap. No app download. No QR code friction. The tourist's phone opens your page instantly. They are already in the city, already deciding where to go.

Your dashboard shows total taps, time of day, which routes convert best, and a live comparison to what the same traffic would have cost on Google Ads.
